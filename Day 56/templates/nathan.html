These are elements that indicate how things look like which font is used , the italicization,alignment, and also attributes

From Pro Archia by Cicero

Nam ceterae (I used the <span> tag here) neque temporum sunt neque aetatum omnium neque locorum: haec studia adulescentiam alunt, senectutem oblectant, secundas res ornant, adversis perfugium ac solacium praebent, delectant domi, non impediunt foris, pernoctant nobiscum, peregrinantur, rusticantur.

"For the others are timeless and ageless and placeless: these writings incite the youth, entertain in old age, they decorate the shelves of our home, they offer safety and refuge in adverse times, they delight at home, they don't impede abroad, they stay with us through the night, they range abroad, they stay in country."

There are two types of HTML Elements: Inline and Block.
Inline elements take up the same space as their content.
Elements are displayed in line, from left to right.
Elements will only wrap when items cannot fit. Examples include <a>, <span>, and <strong>

Block elements are the same height as content, same width as container.
A block element always startson a new line
Examples include <p>, <h1>, <article>, and <section>
